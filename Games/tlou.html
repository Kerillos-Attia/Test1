The last of us

The Last of Us plot revolves around the characters of Joel and Ellie. The duo is a survivor of an apocalypse and players first meet the characters 20 years after a pandemic takes over 60% population The Last of Us is a classic 'Let's Survive a Zombie apocalypse' scenario. Players meet the central characters of the story in 2033.the zombie-making virus is explained to be fungal-based. The political and economical structure of society has collapsed.

The main crux of the game is how Joel and Ellie survive together in a modern pandemic which turns humans to zombies. the reason why Joel needs to escort Ellie to the safety is: that she holds a possible cure in her blood which can make a vaccine and save humankind. The sense of being a saviour of humanity can also be seen taking over Ellie in some points of the game but is sharply humbled down by Joel's serious antics. Both the central characters of the game can be seen fighting off zombies, surviving evil human beings ,and coming together as friends, which allows Joel to understand hope, love, and recovery from the loss of his daughter's life.
